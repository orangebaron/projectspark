Our Goal

  • To empower children in India and our home state of New Jersey.
  • To inspire a love of learning in students.
  • To help fill the world's need for programmers.

What We Do

  • We provide schools in India with computers and internet access.
  • We equip Indian teachers with the knowledge needed to use technology to teach.
  • We help Indian teachers teach programming.
  • We manage and teach a summer programming class in New Jersey.
